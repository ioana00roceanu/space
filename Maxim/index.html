Project Fibonacci aims to develope a outer space community by building a sustainable settlement.

Why that? Keep scrolling to find out!

Don't be afraid of the future. Evolution is a necessity!

Who knows? This may become at some point a back-up plan for humanity if Earth fails overcome its ever growing problems.

More than simply supporting life in space, Project Fibonacci intends to show humanity that steps towards sustainable settlements in space no longer represent a science-fiction concept and can become a reality.

Economy, industry and development in general are not bound to Earth, but can flourish on our space station, grace to mining of resources on asteroids and Mars soil, microgravity industry, and unlimited development opportunities in many scientific fields.

Fibonacci is the name we choose to support the idea that mankind is able to surpass the limits set by nature, and extend its grasp deeper into the universe.

Fibonacci will offer a development site for industries, such as ore refining industry, construction industry, jewelry industry and tourism industry. Space provides an as yet unexploited cradle for science and innovation in the mentioned fields

How to build the future

Structure and Location

Fibonacci settlement has a complex structure with two tori coonnected by a main circular, due to its advantages.

Life support system

The life support system consist of water management, food, gravity, atmosphere, illumiation and recycling.

Economy

Activities of the society which concerns the production, distribution, exchange, and consumption of goods and services, in their interdependence, forms the economy.

IT System

Our space station is supported by the most advanced technology that coordinates the everyday life and also the movement of the structure.

Society

Every single person living here on the settlement will have his/hers worth and will contribute to the good of the station from several points of view.
